Challenge: Sammy the Picky Eater

Sammy is a picky eater, who is on a nuts shopping spree for the coming winter. He goes onto his favorite online store Squirrel Mart, and is overwhelmed by the hundreds of options!

Help Sammy determine which nuts he should consider purchasing by printing out all nuts with a rating of 4 stars or more.
